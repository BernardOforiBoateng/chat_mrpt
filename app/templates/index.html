Welcome to ChatMRPT

To provide you with the best malaria risk analysis experience, we collect and process certain information during your session.

  • Session Data: Temporary session ID, timestamps, and language preferences
  • Interaction Data: Your messages, analysis requests, and system responses
  • Upload Data: File metadata (names, types, sizes) for your CSV and shapefile uploads
  • Analysis Data: Variables selected, calculations performed, and results generated
  • Technical Data: Browser type and basic usage patterns for improving the tool

  • Service Delivery: To process your malaria risk analysis and generate reports
  • User Support: To help you when you encounter issues or need assistance
  • Improvement: To enhance our algorithms and user experience
  • Research: To understand malaria risk patterns and improve public health outcomes

We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

  • Data Export: You can download all your analysis results at any time
  • No Account Required: We don't require personal information or registration

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS

  • We follow industry best practices for data protection
  • Access to interaction logs is restricted to authorized personnel only

What is TPR Analysis?

TPR (Test Positivity Rate) analysis calculates the percentage of positive malaria tests out of total tests conducted. This helps identify high-burden areas for targeted interventions.

Formula: TPR = (Positive Cases / Total Tested) Ã— 100

For each ward, we use: max(RDT positive, Microscopy positive) / max(RDT tested, Microscopy tested)
